# examples — Before / After sample library

> A library of canonical LinkedIn Speak outputs — spanning trivial acts, negative/absurd inputs, career meltdowns, announcements, existential prompts, Chinese-forward samples, other non-English (English-out) cases, and literary source material. Use these as **voice calibration**, not as templates to copy verbatim.
>
> **How to read**: pick the **1–2 samples closest to the current input** (short vs long, positive vs negative, trivial vs milestone). Do **not** read the whole file.
>
> **Forward shape**: **1–2 emoji immediately after the body**, **then** hashtags — **never** emoji after the last `#` (see `style-guide.md` §0 Engine 5 / §5). If a sample row predates this rule, still apply `SKILL.md`.
