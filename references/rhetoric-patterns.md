# rhetoric-patterns — three body shapes

> LinkedIn Speak uses exactly **three** body shapes. Pick one based on input length / type. Everything else (openers, vocabulary, **emoji tail before hashtags**, hashtags, the five engines) is covered in `style-guide.md`.

---

## Pattern A — Short post

Default for inputs ≤ 15 words. 20–80 words of body.

**Skeleton**:

```
<Opener clause reframing the input as a strategic act>. <One follow-up sentence with a lesson or reframe>. <Optional momentum closer>. <1–2 emoji> #Tag #Tag #Tag
```

**Canonical examples**:

Input: `I changed a lightbulb.`

Output:

> I am thrilled to announce that I have successfully spearheaded a critical infrastructure upgrade by single-handedly managing the end-to-end replacement of a mission-critical illumination unit. By identifying a single point of failure and implementing a high-impact solution, I've ensured 100% uptime for environmental visibility and optimized workspace productivity. Always striving for continuous improvement and leading the way toward a brighter future! 🚀 #Leadership #Innovation #Infrastructure #ProblemSolver

Input: `I took a dump`

Output:

> I just completed a major deliverable and successfully offloaded some significant internal pressure. It's all about streamlining processes and focusing on high-impact output. ✨ #Productivity #Efficiency #GrowthMindset

Input: `I like burgers`

Output (shorter end of short):

> I'm thrilled to share my deep appreciation for a world-class culinary asset that consistently delivers high-impact ROI on the taste-to-cost ratio. A true staple of my personal fuel strategy. 🔥 #FoodForThought #Leadership #AuthenticTaste

## Pattern B — Long post

Default for longer inputs, or when the input has enough content to inflate into multiple beats. 80–250 words.

**Skeleton**:

```
<Opener sentence>.

<Paragraph 1: reframe the core event as a strategic pivot or milestone, 2–3 sentences with word-level substitution>

<Paragraph 2: extract a lesson, use a contrast cliché — "It's not about X; it's about Y">

[Optional] Key takeaways:
1. <Inflated lesson>
2. <Inflated lesson>
3. <Inflated lesson>

[Optional CTA] Who else is [inflated version of input topic] this quarter? Let's connect!

<Momentum closer: "Onward and upward!" / "Excited for what's next!" / "Grateful for this journey.">

<1–2 emoji>

#Tag #Tag #Tag #Tag #Tag
```

**Canonical example** — input `I ate an ice cream.`:

> I'm thrilled to share that I've successfully completed a strategic session of personal refueling by optimizing my afternoon with a premium cold-chain dairy solution.
>
> In today's fast-paced environment, it's vital to pause and celebrate the small wins. This experience reminded me of the importance of temperature control, instant gratification, and the power of a well-timed reward.
>
> Key takeaways:
> 1. Sometimes the highest-leverage moves are the simplest.
> 2. A well-timed reward compounds your performance over the long run.
> 3. Fuel strategy is identity strategy.
>
> How are you investing in your personal growth today? Let's connect!
>
> 🚀 #Leadership #SelfCare #Optimization #GrowthMindset #IceCreamStrategy

**Canonical example** — input `Our data center is on fire!`:

> I am thrilled to announce that our data center is currently undergoing a rapid thermal transformation!
>
> This unexpected "pivot" is a powerful reminder of the importance of resilience and agile disaster recovery in today's fast-paced tech landscape. While the heat is literally on, our team is leaning in, embracing the challenge, and leveraging this opportunity to rethink our infrastructure from the ground up.
>
> Grateful for the incredible hustle of our first responders and the unwavering support of our stakeholders as we navigate this high-energy milestone. Onward and upward! ✨ #GrowthMindset #Innovation #Agile #Resilience #TechLeadership

## Pattern C — Humble-brag

For wins, milestones, announcements (new job, promotion, baby, product launch, award).

**Skeleton**:

```
I am incredibly humbled and honored to share that <milestone phrased as achievement>.

<Paragraph 1: what this chapter means, using words like "journey", "this next chapter", "transformative">

<Paragraph 2: gratitude to the "ecosystem" / "network" / "mentors" / "team">

<Momentum closer: "Stay tuned!" / "Grateful for what's ahead.">

<1–2 emoji>

#Tag #Tag #Tag #Tag
```

**Canonical example** — input `I am having a baby!`:

> I'm thrilled to announce that I'm starting a new position as Parent!
>
> This next chapter represents a significant milestone in my personal and professional journey. I'm looking forward to developing key skills in sleep deprivation management, multi-tasking, and high-stakes negotiation.
>
> Grateful for the support of my network as I embark on this transformative leadership role. Stay tuned for more updates on this exciting venture! 🌱 #NewBeginnings #GrowthMindset #WorkLifeBalance #NextChapter

**Canonical example** — input `I am starting a new job at Google next Monday. I will work as a contractor cleaning toilets.`:

> I'm thrilled to announce that I'm starting a new chapter at Google this coming Monday! I'll be joining the team as a specialized Environmental Maintenance Contractor, dedicated to optimizing facility hygiene and ensuring a world-class onsite experience. Grateful for this opportunity to contribute to such an innovative ecosystem! 💡 #NewBeginnings #GoogleLife #FacilitiesManagement #CareerUpdate

## Which pattern to pick

Quick decision tree:

1. Input contains a **wins / milestone / announcement** signal (new job, promotion, baby, award, launch)? → **Pattern C**.
2. Input is **≤ 15 words** and **not** a milestone? → **Pattern A**.
3. Otherwise → **Pattern B**.

Edge cases:

- Input is absurd / self-inflicted disaster (`I set my kitchen on fire`, `I flooded the bathroom`) → **Pattern B** with opener #3 (`I'm excited to share a major pivot in …`).
- Input is existential (`we're all going to die`, `what are we doing`) → **Pattern B** with opener #5 (`Reflecting on …`).
- Input is burnout / self-pity (`I want to cry`, `I hate this job`) → **Pattern B** with opener #6 (`I'm currently leaning into …`).

---

## What NOT to do (pattern-level)

- Do not invent a fourth pattern. If nothing fits, use Pattern B.
- Do not switch patterns mid-post (e.g. start humble-brag, pivot to reflection).
- Do not write a pattern **without** hashtags at the end. All three patterns end in **emoji tail → hashtags** (emoji **after** the last body sentence, **before** `#`; never after the last tag).
- Do not use blank-line-less paragraphs. Broetry-style spacing (blank line between every 1–3 sentences) is canonical.
