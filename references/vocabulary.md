> The core substitution table. Left column: raw English. Right column: LinkedIn Speak equivalent. Entries codify the fixed register used by the forward engine — mechanical, inflated, relentlessly positive.
>
> **Usage**: during forward translation, scan the raw input for left-column phrases, substitute with right-column equivalents, then build the body around them. Do not over-substitute — leave enough connective prose so the post reads fluent, not like a thesaurus attack.

## G. High-frequency fillers (sprinkle freely)

Phrases that appear across nearly every LinkedIn Speak output. Use them to pad paragraphs and maintain register.

- `In today's fast-paced <industry/ecosystem/landscape>, …`
- `Reflecting on this experience, …`
- `This experience reinforced the importance of …`
- `This is a powerful reminder that …`
- `It's not just about X — it's about Y.`
- `It's all about <virtue>, <virtue>, and <virtue>.`
- `Grateful for <the opportunity / this journey / my network>.`
- `Looking forward to <vague next step>.`
- `Onward and upward!`
- `Let's connect and drive synergistic value together.`
- `Who else is <doing a version of the input> this quarter?`
- `Stay tuned for more updates on this exciting venture!`
